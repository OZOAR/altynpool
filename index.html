about us

Altyn pool was created by the miners community. We have a precise understanding of network decentralization importance, as well as the realization that Kazakhstan is becoming a key region in mining. Local pool should take the industry to the new level and provide miners from all over the world with easy access to the key advantages of the region.

We are engaged not only in mining, but also in software development in the field of mining and cryptocurrencies. We do not intend to rest on our accomplishments and are constantly working to improve our products.

Altyn pool team uses unique expertise and vast experience in order to provide miners with uninterrupted and trouble-free mining, as well as a convenient entry point for cooperation with Kazakhstan.

our services

Mining advisory

We provide consulting services for miners, data centers and investors. We will help to find the best solution for any of your requests and we will also assist in finding investors and attracting miners to your data center. Thanks to the large network, we can always find the necessary competencies.

Mining pool

Our qualified team uses the world's best solutions to ensure stable work and high like-rate. A wide network of servers around the world, including in Kazakhstan, as well as round-the-clock support will increase not only profit, but usability also.

Firmware

We develop unique firmware configurations for the most popular ASICs in order to improve mining efficiency and reduce electricity and maintenance costs. Our team can create a firmware that will meet your request exactly.

Mining farms

Large contacts network allows us to offer you special conditions for the equipment placement both in Kazakhstan and around the world. In addition, we are always open for cooperation with data centers to attract miners for the relocation purpose.

why choose altyn pool?

most popular coins

Altyn pool supports the main mining algorithms: SHA-256, Scrypt and Equihash. We are constantly analyzing the mining market to further increase the number of available coins.

Merged mining

Merged mining allows two or more cryptocurrencies to be mined at the same time without compromising overall mining.

Fair payouts

Altyn Pool supports the most reliable payment methods: FPPS and PPLNS and also provides the option of automatic payments once a day with an adjustable withdrawal threshold.

Local support

As a support for our clients from Kazakhstan, we set zero mining fees. Our pool does not charge any fees from Kazakh miners. To get special conditions, leave a request through the form below.

Vip service

Our value is our customers. We provide a whole range of services and privileges for large miners. For ease of use, a personal manager will be with you 24/7.
